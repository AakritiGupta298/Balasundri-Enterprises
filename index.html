About Us

We, at Balasundri Enterprises, have started working under our visionary mentor, Mr. Sanjeev Gupta, who aspires to cement a respectful name of his company deep into the Indian markets. Our sheer purpose is to enhance the satisfactory experience of customers for a vast range of unique & high-quality home/office furnishing accessories. For this, we are working effectively as a manufacturer and wholesaler. The product line which we are offering to our customers ranges from Pen Stands, Dustbins, Serving Trays, Bucket Bags, Photo Frames to many more. These items are designed and developed by our professionals who possess expertise in effortlessly manufacturing a detailed and superior quality product range.
